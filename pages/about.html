About

My name is Tim Henderson. I am a researcher and software engineer at Google. I recieved a PhD in Computer Science from Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio in 2017. My research interests include data mining, software engineering, software testing, code clones, fault localization and program analysis. In the past I ran CWRU Hacker Society and attended a number of programming clubs in the Cleveland area.
